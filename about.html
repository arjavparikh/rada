Hello!
I am Radhika. I independently run my design studio.

My brand vision is to create impactful design that creatively addresses brand-recognition, by means of Identity Design and Packaging Design.

Our common goal remains the same: to create a great piece of communication. With meticulousness in my approach, a need to know more, my involvement and patience, I honestly invest my time and efforts to understand your brand, your vision, your business objectives and partnering you with my creative process from the initial stages itself.

Identity Design

Simplicity and Sustainability are the two key components of an identity. When done right, branding becomes a successful implementation of design, language and experience, all combining to consistency and clarity. And on the chaotic web, in 2017, branding has become the key to cutting through the clutter.

Packaging Design

Packaging is the last message a consumer sees and a last chance to convince him to buy your product. From a shopper's point of view, a product is never seen alone. So you do want it to have the a greater "shelf-impact.
